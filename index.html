A $10 deposit is required to secure your appointment. It is non-refundable and applied toward your total cost. Your appointment is not confirmed until the deposit has been received and manually verified.

2. Appointment Confirmation

Submitting a booking request puts your time slot in "Pending" status. You will receive confirmation once your deposit proof has been reviewed. If payment is not received within 30 minutes, your slot will be released.

3. Rescheduling & Cancellations

Reschedule at least 24 hours in advance to transfer your deposit. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before or no-shows forfeit the deposit.

4. Late Policy

Clients arriving more than 15 minutes late may have their appointment cancelled and the deposit will not be refunded.
